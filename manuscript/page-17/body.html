And this man whom we realise does not impress us unfavourably; if he is without charm, he is surely immensely interesting and attractive; he is so strong in his intellectual convictions, he is so free from intellectual affectations, he is such an ingenuous egotist, so naovely human; he is so mercilessly honest and independent, and, at times (one may be permitted to think), so mistaken.
